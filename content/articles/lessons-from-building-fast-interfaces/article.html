Fast interfaces are often the result of restraint rather than clever tricks.

Reduce what the browser has to do

The most reliable performance work usually comes from fewer scripts, simpler layouts, and less work on initial render.

Use motion with intent

Animations should clarify transitions, not compete with them. Small hover states and subtle fades often do more than large page effects.

Build pages that can stand still

When the structure and typography are strong, the interface still feels polished even before interactive enhancements load.
